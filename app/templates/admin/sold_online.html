How it works

When a card sells on TCGPlayer, the email receiver flags it here automatically (you can also flag one manually below). Flagged items show a Sold Online overlay in the POS and cannot be added to a cart.

Confirm shipped once you've pulled and shipped the card: it's removed from inventory and the quantity update is pushed to your other channels. Use False alarm if the flag was wrong — that clears it with no inventory change. Unresolved flags auto-expire at the end of the following calendar day.
