How it works

When a card sells on TCGPlayer or eBay before the CSV sync updates inventory, flag it here to prevent a double-sale at the register. Flagged items show a Sold Online overlay in the POS and cannot be added to a cart. Flags auto-expire at the end of the following calendar day — or dismiss manually once the inventory is reconciled.

Future: wire an email parser to POST /admin/sold-online/signal with card_name, channel, and order_id fields.
