Step through two end-to-end scenarios showing how a single card moves through TCGPlayer, the local DB, eBay, and Shopify POS.

Legend & notes
  • Active — system is currently doing work in this step
  • Done — system has completed its part for this scenario
  • Idle — system is not involved in this step
  • Animated red arrows show the data flowing in the current step. Faded labels show paths not active right now.
  • Note: every product (cards, sealed, supplies) is published to Shopify as a POS-channel-only catalog product so the in-store inventory always reflects the DB. Cards/sealed live on all three channels (TCGPlayer + eBay + Shopify in-store); supplies are Shopify-only.
  • Why the new "publish + qty push" arrow: this is the path that keeps the in-store cashier's inventory accurate. If a card sells on eBay, the next Shopify push removes it from the in-store catalog before the next walk-in customer can attempt to buy it.
